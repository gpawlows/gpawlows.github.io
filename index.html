Geoff Pawlowski's Biography


My name is Geoff Pawlowski and I was born in Manassas, VA sometime a little over 40 years ago! I have a mother, father, and brother who I all love dearly and miss on a regular basis because they still live in the Northern Virginia suburbs. I grew up in Virginia and vent to college at Virginia Tech where I made many friendships that I still carry with me today


These days, I live with my wife, Jeanette, my two daughters, Lucy and Emme, and our pet dog, Tileh in sunny San Diego, CA. I work full time, go to school at night, and parent quite a bit in between, so that doesn't leave much time for hobbies right now! One day, I hope to get back to hiking more often and seeing if I can shoot in the 80's on the golf course again:)
